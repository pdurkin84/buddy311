Empowering citizen engagement. Streamlining government response.

Buddy311 is the simplest way to report a problem in your city - period. Skip the confusing menus and tell the city what's wrong by talking naturally into your phone. Buddy311 also supports cities through AI and big data, helping governments spend less time processing requests and more time fixing problems for their residents.

buddy311 is Here to Help You

JUST SPEAK YOUR MIND

Reporting a problem in your city is as simple as talking into your phone. Buddy311 creates a ticket with your speech and notifies your local government.

AUTOMATED CLASSIFICATION

Users no longer need to wade through multiple menus trying to decide which category their problem fits into. Buddy311 does the heavy lifting for you, and chooses the appropriate category based on your problem.

EFFICIENT INSIGHTS

Government 311 services spend less time rerouting misclassified tickets, and more time solving problems.
